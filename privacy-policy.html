Privacy Policy

Paseo Property Management · Last updated: July 15, 2026

Paseo Property Management ("Paseo," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect from tenants, property owners, and website visitors, how we use it, and the choices you have. By providing your information to us or using our services, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact details such as your name, mailing address, email address, and mobile phone number;
  • Information you provide when signing a lease, completing a move-in form, or communicating with us about your tenancy or property;
  • Payment and account information necessary to administer rent, deposits, and owner disbursements;
  • Maintenance requests and related communications.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Manage your tenancy or ownership relationship, including leasing, rent, maintenance, and account communications;
  • Send you service messages you have requested or consented to, such as maintenance updates, emergency property alerts, rent reminders, and account communications;
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support;
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations.

Mobile Information & SMS

We do not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers or SMS opt-in consent with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third parties.

We use your mobile number only to send tenancy-related messages you have consented to receive — maintenance updates, emergency property alerts, rent reminders, and account communications. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP, or contact us using the details below. Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of renting or of any service.

How We Share Information

We may share your information with service providers who perform services on our behalf (such as payment processing, accounting, and maintenance vendors), with property owners as necessary to administer their properties, and where required by law or to protect our legal rights. As stated above, we never sell or share mobile numbers or SMS consent for marketing purposes.

Data Retention & Security

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy and to comply with our legal obligations. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information.

Your Choices

You may update your contact information or opt out of non-essential communications by contacting us. You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
